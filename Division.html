Technologies and Platforms

Oracle

Oracle is a leading provider of database software and technology, cloud-engineered systems, and enterprise software products.

SAP

SAP is a global leader in enterprise resource planning (ERP) software, helping businesses manage operations and customer relations.

Net Suite

NetSuite is a software company that has developed a cloud-based business management platform used by more than 42,000 fast-growing organizations across the globe. That platform consists of a suite of applications that help companies run their business, understand the performance of their businesses and drive major efficiency gains and cost savings.
